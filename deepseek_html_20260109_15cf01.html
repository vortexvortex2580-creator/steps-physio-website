Our Treatment Approaches

Injury Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of sports injuries using functional movement screening and diagnostic tools to create targeted treatment plans.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilization, soft tissue massage, and myofascial release to reduce pain and improve mobility.

Recovery Modalities

Utilizing cutting-edge equipment like shockwave therapy, ultrasound, and electrical stimulation to accelerate healing and reduce inflammation.

Performance Enhancement

Biomechanical analysis and sport-specific training to optimize movement patterns and improve athletic performance while minimizing injury risk.

About Our Clinic

Expert Care for Active Individuals

Founded by a team of sports medicine specialists, Steps Physio Performance Lab is dedicated to helping athletes and active individuals recover from injuries and reach their performance potential.

Our multidisciplinary team includes physiotherapists, sports therapists, and strength coaches who work together to provide comprehensive care tailored to your specific needs and goals.

We combine evidence-based practice with the latest rehabilitation technologies to deliver exceptional outcomes for our patients, from weekend warriors to professional athletes.
